Skillura is an innovative platform tailored for agencies aiming to optimize their hiring and training processes. By streamlining employee vetting, Skillura ensures that candidates meet your standards, while its intelligent training tools facilitate rapid onboarding and development. The platform offers customizable testing, seamless onboarding, and analytics to track progress, enabling data-driven decisions to enhance team performance. With Skillura, agencies can reduce hiring and training costs by up to 90%, achieving a more efficient and effective workforce.

## Features

- **Exams and Interview Process**: Real-time Interviews and Skill-based Exams for hiring and training process of an agency.
- **Comprehensive Assessments**: Utilize skill-based exams and real-time interviews to effectively vet candidates and ensure they meet your standards.
- **Customizable Pipelines**: Design tailored onboarding and training pathways to align with your agency's specific needs.
- **Progress Tracking and Analytics**: Monitor employee development with advanced analytics, enabling data-driven decisions to enhance team performance.
- **Messaging**: Enable seamless communication between candidates, recruiters, and trainers with an integrated messaging system.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React with Redux for state management, ensuring a smooth and scalable user experience.
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express for handling server-side logic efficiently.
- **Database**: MongoDB for managing user data and tutorial content.
- **Styling**: Material UI (MUI) for modern, accessible, responsive, and component-based design with a touch of the best - CSS.
- **Animations**: Framer Motion for smooth and interactive UI animations.
- **File Uploads**: Cloudinary for seamless file, video, and image storage and management.
